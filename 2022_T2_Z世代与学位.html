Is a Degree Still Worth It? Z 世代与学位价值

2022 年英语（一）真题 · Text 2 · 约 420 词 · 建议做题 18 min · 话题：教育×职场（社论评述）
正确答案：26C 27B 28C 29D 30A题型：建议+细节+例证+细节+推断结论难度：中等（选项同义替换密集）

01原文精读

🟡 黄色高亮 = 重难点词/短语，点击右侧出卡片 ｜ 蓝色虚线 = 长难句，点击跳到精析 ｜ 每段「译」= 段落译文 ｜ 选中任意其他单词浮出「查有道」

①As the latest crop of students pen their undergraduate application form and weigh up their options, it may be worth considering just how the point, purpose and value of a degree has changed and what Generation Z need to consider as they start the third stage of their educational journey.

当新一批学生填写本科申请表、权衡各自的选择时，值得思考的是：学位的意义、目的与价值已经发生了怎样的变化，以及 Z 世代在开启教育旅程第三阶段时需要考虑什么。

②Millennials were told that if you did well in school, got a decent degree, you would be set up for life. But that promise has been found wanting. As degrees became universal, they became devalued. Education was no longer a secure route of social mobility. Today, 28 per cent of graduates in the UK are in non-graduate roles, a percentage which is double the average among OECD countries.

千禧一代曾被告知：只要在学校表现好、拿个像样的学位，你这辈子就稳了。但这个承诺已被证明靠不住。随着学位的普及，它也随之贬值。教育不再是社会流动的可靠通道。如今，英国 28% 的毕业生从事的是不需要学位的岗位，这一比例是 OECD 国家平均水平的两倍。

③This is not to say that there is no point in getting a degree, but rather stress that a degree is not for everyone, that the switch from classroom to lecture hall is not an inevitable one and that other options are available.

这并不是说读学位毫无意义，而是想强调：学位并不适合每个人，从（中学）教室走进（大学）阶梯教室并非必然之路，还有其他选择存在。

④Thankfully, there are signs that this is already happening, with Generation Z seeking to learn from their millennial predecessors, even if parents and teachers tend to be still set in the degree mindset. Employers have long seen the advantages of hiring school leavers who often prove themselves to be more committed and loyal employees than graduates. Many too are seeing the advantages of scrapping a degree requirement for certain roles.

值得庆幸的是，有迹象表明这种转变已经在发生：Z 世代正试图从千禧一代前辈身上吸取教训——尽管父母和老师往往仍然固守"学位思维"。雇主们早就看到了雇用中学毕业生的好处：他们往往证明自己比大学毕业生更尽职、更忠诚。许多雇主还看到了取消某些岗位学位门槛的好处。

⑤For those for whom a degree is the desired route, consider that this may well be the first of many. In this age of generalists, it pays to have specific knowledge or skills. Postgraduates now earn 40 per cent more than graduates. When more and more of us have a degree, it makes sense to have two.

对于那些确实想走学位路线的人：要考虑到这可能只是许多个学位中的第一个。在这个通才遍地的时代，拥有专门的知识或技能是划算的。如今研究生比本科毕业生多挣 40%。当越来越多的人都有学位时，拥有两个学位才说得通。

⑥It is unlikely that Generation Z will be done with education at 18 or 21; they will need to be constantly up-skilling throughout their career to stay employable. It has been estimated that this generation, due to the pressures of technology, the wish for personal fulfilment and desire for diversity, will work for 17 different employers over the course of their working life and have five different careers. Education, and not just knowledge gained on campus, will be a core part of Generation Z's career trajectory.

Z 世代不太可能在 18 岁或 21 岁就与教育"两清"；为了保持就业能力，他们需要在整个职业生涯中不断提升技能。据估计，由于技术压力、对个人成就感的渴望和对多样性的追求，这代人一生将为 17 个不同的雇主工作、经历 5 种不同的职业。教育——而不仅仅是在校园里获得的知识——将成为 Z 世代职业轨迹的核心组成部分。

⑦Older generations often talk about their degree in the present and personal tense: 'I am a geographer' or 'I am a classist'. Their sons or daughters would never say such a thing; it's as if they already know that their degree won't define them in the same way.

老一辈谈起自己的学位时常用现在时、且与自我绑定："我是地理学者""我是古典学学者"。他们的子女绝不会这样说——仿佛他们早已明白，学位不会再以同样的方式定义他们。

02题目与解析（点选项即判 · 再看解析）

建议/主旨26. The author suggests that Generation Z should ______.

[A] be careful in choosing a college
[B] be diligent at each educational stage
[C] reassess the necessity of college education
[D] postpone their undergraduate application
答案 C　定位①段 it may be worth considering just how the point, purpose and value of a degree has changed + ③段三连 that：a degree is not for everyone / not an inevitable one / other options are available —— 重新想清楚"要不要上大学" = reassess the necessity。
干扰项：A 偷换——作者讨论的是"要不要读"，不是"选哪所"；B "每个阶段都努力"心灵鸡汤，无中生有；D "推迟申请"曲解 weigh up options（权衡≠延期）。
技巧：问 author suggests 的题，去找作者施加"该做什么"语气的句子（worth considering / consider that / stress that）。

细节27. The percentage of UK graduates in non-graduate roles reflects ______.

[A] Millennials' opinions about work
[B] the shrinking value of a degree
[C] public discontent with education
[D] the desired route of social mobility
答案 B　数字必有用途：②段逻辑链 As degrees became universal, they became devalued → no longer a secure route → 28% 的数据就是"学位贬值"的证据。devalued → shrinking value，教科书级同义替换。
干扰项：A 张冠李戴——"被告知会 set up for life"是千禧代听到的承诺，不是他们对工作的看法；C "公众不满"无中生有；D 反向——原文说教育不再是 secure route。
技巧：问"数据说明什么"→ 答案永远是数据前后的观点句，不是数据本身。

细节28. The author considers it a good sign that ______.

[A] Generation Z are seeking to earn a decent degree
[B] school leavers are willing to be skilled workers
[C] employers are taking a realistic attitude to degrees
[D] parents are changing their minds about education
答案 C　定位④段 Thankfully（好迹象信号词）：Employers have long seen the advantages of hiring school leavers + the advantages of scrapping a degree requirement —— 雇主不迷信学位、按岗位实际需要用人 = 对学位持务实态度（realistic attitude）。
干扰项：A 反向——好迹象恰恰是 Z 世代明白学位非必需；B 偷换——是雇主爱雇 school leavers，不是他们自己"愿意当技术工人"；D 反向——父母 still set in the degree mindset（还没转变）。
技巧：even if / 让步从句里的内容（父母老师没变）常被拿来做反向干扰项。

细节29. It is advised in Paragraph 5 that those with one degree should ______.

[A] make an early decision on their career
[B] attend on-the-job training programs
[C] team up with high-paid postgraduates
[D] further their studies in a specific field
答案 D　⑤段：this may well be the first of many（第一个学位而已）+ it pays to have specific knowledge or skills + Postgraduates now earn 40 per cent more + it makes sense to have two —— 继续读、往"专"里读 = further their studies in a specific field。
干扰项：A "尽早定职业"无中生有；B 在职培训是⑥段的 up-skilling，段落错位（题干明确限定 Paragraph 5）；C 把"研究生多挣40%"曲解成"和高薪研究生组队"，搞笑项。
技巧：题干限定段落时，其他段落的正确信息也是错误选项——29B 就是专门为不看题干限定的人挖的坑。

推断结论30. What can be concluded about Generation Z from the last two paragraphs?

[A] Lifelong learning will define them.
[B] They will make qualified educators.
[C] Degrees will no longer appeal to them.
[D] They will have a limited choice of jobs.
答案 A　⑥段 constantly up-skilling throughout their career + Education ... will be a core part of Generation Z's career trajectory，⑦段 their degree won't define them in the same way —— 合起来：定义他们的不再是一纸学位，而是贯穿一生的学习 = Lifelong learning will define them（A 与⑦段原文形成漂亮的呼应）。
干扰项：B "成为合格的教育者"无中生有（education≠educator）；C 过度推断——⑤段还建议读第二个学位，学位并非"不再有吸引力"；D 反向——17 个雇主、5 种职业 = 选择更多不是更少。
技巧：结论题选项常把原文关键词重组：B 用 education 变 educator 就是典型的"词形陷阱"。

03长难句精析（正文蓝色虚线句）

As the latest crop of students pen their undergraduate application form and weigh up their options, it may be worth considering just how the point, purpose and value of a degree has changed and what Generation Z need to consider as they start the third stage of their educational journey.
  • As 引导时间状语从句，从句内 pen 和 weigh up 两个谓语并列（pen 在此是动词"填写"！）
  • it is worth doing：it 形式主语 + worth 句型
  • considering 后接两个并列宾语从句：how...（学位价值如何变化）and what...（Z世代要考虑什么）——26 题题干就化用了这两个从句
  • the third stage：中学之后的"第三阶段"，即高等教育
译：当新一批学生填写本科申请表、权衡选择时，值得思考：学位的意义、目的与价值已如何变化，以及 Z 世代开启教育第三阶段时需要考虑什么。
This is not to say that there is no point in getting a degree, but rather stress that a degree is not for everyone, that the switch from classroom to lecture hall is not an inevitable one and that other options are available.
  • This is not to say A, but rather (to) stress B：先排除误读再立论——写作与阅读都极高频的"让步-立论"结构
  • stress 后三个 that 从句排比：不是人人需要 / 并非必然 / 另有选择——26 题答案的完整证据链
  • the switch from classroom to lecture hall：借代——从中学教室到大学阶梯教室 = 上大学
译：这不是说读学位毫无意义，而是要强调：学位不适合所有人，升入大学并非必然，其他选择依然存在。
It has been estimated that this generation, due to the pressures of technology, the wish for personal fulfilment and desire for diversity, will work for 17 different employers over the course of their working life and have five different careers.
  • It has been estimated that：it 形式主语 + 被动语态（"据估计"），写作图表描述可直接搬
  • due to 引出三个并列原因（技术压力 / 个人成就 / 多样性渴望），插入在主语和谓语之间——先跳过读主干
  • 主干：this generation will work for 17 employers and have five careers
译：据估计，由于技术压力、对个人成就感的渴望和对多样性的追求，这代人一生将为 17 个雇主工作、经历 5 种职业。

05可搬进作文/翻译的素材

This is not to say that ..., but rather ...
"这不是说……而是……" —— 议论文让步反驳神句，先堵住误读再亮观点：This is not to say technology is useless, but rather that it must be used wisely.
But that promise has been found wanting.
"这个承诺被证明靠不住" —— found wanting（发现有欠缺）替换 proved unreliable，短句转折有力。
it pays to do ...
"做……是划算的" —— It pays to plan ahead. 三词开头，简洁地道。
It has been estimated that ...
"据估计……" —— 图表作文引数据标配；同族：It is reported/predicted that...
stay employable / up-skill / career trajectory
就业话题词打包：保持就业能力 / 提升技能 / 职业轨迹——教育与就业类大作文的核心弹药。
be set in the ... mindset
"固守……思维" —— Many remain set in the exam-oriented mindset. 批判守旧观念可用。
X won't define them
"X 不会定义他们" —— 与 2022 T1 的 define 呼应：A single test score won't define who you are.（跨篇复现词优先记）
