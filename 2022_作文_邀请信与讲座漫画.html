A小作文 · 邀请信

Write an email to a professor at a British university, inviting him/her to organize a team for the international innovation contest to be held at your university.
You should write about 100 words. Do not use your own name; use "Li Ming" instead. (10 points)

审题三件事

体裁
邀请信（email 体）：称呼 Dear Professor + 姓氏或 Dear Professor,；落款 Yours sincerely, / Li Ming——格式占分，写错先扣
要点
三个必须覆盖：① 发出邀请并自报身份 ② 介绍比赛（名称/地点/为什么请 TA 组队）③ 表达期待、请求回复——漏一个要点掉一档
语域
写给教授 = 正式语体：不用缩写（don't→do not）、不用口语词（a lot of→numerous）、多用情态委婉（would/could）

范文（116 词 · 🟡 亮点点击看讲解）

Dear Professor Smith,

开门见山On behalf of the Students' Union, I am writing to invite you to organize a team for the International Innovation Contest, which is to be held at our university this June.

活动+理由The contest, themed "Technology for a Greener Future", aims to bring together creative minds from different countries. Given your distinguished achievements in engineering, we believe your team's participation would greatly enhance the quality of the event. Each team is expected to have three to five members, and we will cover the accommodation of all invited guests.

期待回复Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact me. We are looking forward to your favorable reply.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

尊敬的史密斯教授：
我谨代表学生会写信邀请您组队参加将于今年六月在我校举办的国际创新大赛。本届大赛以"科技助力绿色未来"为主题，旨在汇聚各国创新人才。鉴于您在工程领域的杰出成就，我们相信贵队的参与将大大提升赛事水准。每支队伍应由三至五人组成，所有受邀嘉宾的住宿由我方承担。如有任何问题，请随时与我联系。期待您的肯定答复。
此致　李明
零分/重扣雷区：① 写自己真名（题目明令 Li Ming，写真名判 0 分风险）；② 称呼写 Dear Professor Smith Professor（叠床架屋）或直呼 Dear Smith；③ email 体不需要写日期和地址；④ 字数严重不足 80 词。

B大作文 · 图画作文

Write an essay of 160–200 words based on the picture below. In your essay, you should 1) describe the picture briefly, 2) interpret the implied meaning, and 3) give your comments. (20 points)
图画内容：两名学生站在"校园讲座"海报前。左边学生说："不是我们专业的，听了也没多大用。"右边学生说："去听听肯定有好处。"

审题：一图两态度

主题
对专业外知识的两种态度：功利短视（只学"有用"的）vs 开放心态（跨界吸收）——落点是大学学习观 / 通识素养
站队
必须旗帜鲜明支持右边学生；第二段批"唯有用论"，第三段给建议
结构
铁三段：①描图（含引用对话）②寓意（对比+论证为什么要跨界学习）③评论（表态+建议）

范文（198 词 · 🟡 亮点点击看讲解）

①描图As is vividly depicted in the cartoon, two students are standing in front of a poster advertising a campus lecture. One of them dismisses it, saying that it has nothing to do with their major and is therefore of little use, while the other holds the opposite view: attending the lecture will surely do them good.

②寓意The two contrasting attitudes reveal a question worth pondering: should learning be confined to one's own specialty? Regrettably, quite a few students take a purely utilitarian approach to knowledge, measuring everything by its immediate usefulness. Such a mindset, however, runs counter to the very purpose of university education. Many breakthroughs are born where disciplines meet; exposure to fields beyond one's major broadens horizons, stimulates fresh thinking and, more often than not, pays off in unexpected ways. What seems "useless" today may well prove invaluable tomorrow.

③评论In my view, college students should embrace every opportunity to learn instead of calculating short-term returns. After all, a lecture outside one's major costs no more than an evening, yet the new perspective it offers may last a lifetime.

正如漫画生动描绘的那样，两名学生站在一张校园讲座海报前。其中一人不以为然，说讲座与他们的专业无关、因此没什么用；另一人则持相反看法：去听讲座肯定有好处。
两种截然相反的态度揭示了一个值得深思的问题：学习应当局限于自己的专业吗？令人遗憾的是，不少学生对知识抱着纯功利的态度，用"眼下是否有用"来衡量一切。然而这种心态与大学教育的根本目的背道而驰。许多突破诞生于学科交汇之处；接触专业之外的领域能开阔视野、激发新思维，而且往往会以意想不到的方式带来回报。今天看似"无用"的东西，明天很可能被证明无比珍贵。
依我之见，大学生应当拥抱每一个学习机会，而不是计算短期回报。毕竟，一场专业外的讲座不过花费一个晚上，它带来的新视角却可能受用终身。
丢分预警：① 第一段忘记把图中对话转述出来（图画细节分）；② 全文只批评不建议（第三要求 give your comments 必须落到建议/展望）；③ 背诵痕迹过重：开头堆砌 "With the rapid development of society..." 这类与图无关的万能句——阅卷负面清单第一名。

03可迁移框架（换题也能用）

邀请信骨架（换活动名即可）

On behalf of ___, I am writing to invite you to ___ , which is to be held at ___ .
身份+目的+活动+时间地点，一句话完成第一段。
Given your distinguished ___ , we believe your participation would greatly enhance ___ .
"为什么请你"的万能理由句——夸得具体（achievements in X）才不像模板。
Should you have any questions, please feel free to ___ . We are looking forward to your favorable reply.
收尾双句：虚拟倒装 + 期待回复，任何书信通用。

图画作文骨架（三段各配一开头）

① As is vividly depicted in the cartoon, ... while ...
描图段：主句摆场景，while 对比两个人物/两种行为，一段搞定。
② The (two contrasting attitudes / scene) reveal(s) a question worth pondering: should ...?
寓意段起手：图 → 问题，比"the picture tells us"高两档；随后 Regrettably, quite a few... / Such a mindset, however, runs counter to ...
③ In my view, ... should ... instead of ... . After all, ...
评论段：表态 + instead of 排除错误做法 + After all 补一个理由收束。
